Our Mission

Every Jew Is a Letter In One Living Torah

The Zohar teaches that every Jewish soul is a letter in the Torah. Just as a Torah scroll is rendered invalid when even a single letter is flawed or missing, no Jewish community is whole when even one voice goes unheard.

A Letter in the Scroll was built on this truth. We give every learner — from the seasoned scholar to the curious beginner — a place to study Torah with depth, warmth, and real friends by their side.

Chavruta Study: Pair or group learning that honors the ancient tradition of studying Torah with a partner — because two voices always illuminate more than one.

Universal Connection: Bookmark and react to verses — your highlights are seen by every chavruta you belong to, building a living map of what moves you.

Lead Together: Promote co-leaders who share the responsibility of guiding a learning circle — because great Torah study is always a shared act.

Holidays, Prayers & Songs: Explore the rhythm of Jewish time — from Shabbat zmirot to Yom Tov, prayers to Parshat HaShavua, all in one place.

"When one letter is missing, the scroll is invalid. When one person is absent, the people are incomplete."

Join a chavruta. Study deeply. Be a letter in the scroll.
